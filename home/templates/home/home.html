{% trans "What's BuyAware?" %}

{% blocktrans %}

We are a group of young students from different regions of Switzerland who met at the Swiss Talent Forum in 2015. It was here that we initiated the BuyAware project, and have been developing the idea ever since.

It is our goal to inform consumers about the ecological and ethical background of different products in of the electronics sector, so that the consumers can make a more responsible purchase decision. In order to do so we assess the devices according to six categories: climate and energy, ecology, worker rights, conflict minerals, transparency of the company and performance. The results of these assessments are then merged to form the product profile which our users can access over our database and use for comparison purposes. It is important to us that we present our assessments in an all-in-one format so that our customers can immediately see where a product has its strengths and weaknesses. Additionally, we understand that different people have different needs, which is why the weightings of our six assessed categories can be adjusted individually, so that we are able to give the most helpful feedback possible.

At the moment, we focus our activities only on smartphones. In the long run however, we are planning on expanding our service to cover all products in the electronics sector.
