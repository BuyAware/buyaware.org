About

We are a group of students from several Swiss universities who have met at the Swiss Talent Forum 2015 in Thun, Switzerland, which is a think-tank organized by Swiss Youth in Science. It was at this event on the topic of Smart Growth & Consumption that we laid the foundation stone for BuyAware.

The objective of BuyAware is to contribute to more responsible consumption by closing information gaps in the field of electronics. By providing background data on consumer goods prior to purchasing, we want to enable more conscious, individual and reflected buying decision.
